## Abstract

Phytocure proposes a decentralized science (DeSci) infrastructure for clinical cannabis medicine, enabling trustless prescription issuance, open peer-reviewed research publication, AI-assisted strain analysis, and community-governed therapeutic protocols on the Solana blockchain. The $PYCURE token (CA: `9jBbyCpEoWa9frvtAbq4xNQ8xoHPedeWYkVBDVU9pump`) serves as the network's coordination and incentive mechanism.
