## Token Utility

### 1. Clinical Prescription Access
- Prescriptions require a $PYCURE staking deposit from licensed practitioners
- Stake acts as quality bond: slashed upon verified malpractice
- Patients pay prescription fees in SOL or $PYCURE

### 2. Research Publication & Peer Review
- Authors deposit $PYCURE to publish research to the on-chain registry
- Peer reviewers earn $PYCURE for validated, high-quality reviews
- Citation rewards: authors earn $PYCURE as their work is cited

### 3. AI Analysis Credits
- Strain analysis engine charges $PYCURE per query
- Bulk API access available through staked subscription plans
- Revenue distributed to stakers and protocol treasury

### 4. Distributor Network
- Verified distributors stake $PYCURE as reputation collateral
- Higher stake = higher trust tier = more product listings
- Slashing mechanism for counterfeit or non-compliant products

### 5. Governance
- 1 $PYCURE = 1 vote in protocol governance proposals
- Minimum 10,000 $PYCURE to submit a governance proposal
- Quadratic voting applied to major protocol upgrades

## Treasury Management

The Protocol Treasury (`5BNvMkDC4eeNzxrUJjgeUU5eSJcSfwPriMcrsuxaphet`) is governed by:
- Multi-signature wallet (3-of-5 keyholders)
- On-chain DAO vote required for disbursements > 50,000 $PYCURE
- Quarterly transparency reports published to the research-data repository
- 10% of all protocol fees auto-routed to treasury

## Anti-Inflation Mechanisms

1. **Buy & Burn:** 25% of all SOL-denominated fees used to market-buy and burn $PYCURE
2. **Emission Schedule:** Research rewards halve every 2 years (similar to Bitcoin halving)
3. **Stake-to-Access:** Core features require staking, reducing circulating supply
